## 2025-02-20 - [Documentation Accessibility and Clarity]
**Learning:** For repositories focused on architecture or documentation, the UX is the documentation itself. Renaming cryptic asset files and embedding them with descriptive alt text significantly improves the "first-look" experience and accessibility.
**Action:** Always check if visual assets are embedded in READMEs and have descriptive alt text. Ensure filenames are clean and maintainable.
