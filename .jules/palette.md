## 2025-05-15 - Accessible Text Alternatives for AI Diagrams
**Learning:** AI-generated diagrams (like the architecture visualization) require text-based alternatives or comprehensive lists to ensure accessibility and mitigate potential reading difficulties caused by image artifacts or generated text typos.
**Action:** In documentation-only repositories, treat the documentation as the UI and ensure every complex visual asset has a corresponding structured text section that replicates its information.
