## 2024-05-14 - Text Alternatives for Complex Diagrams
**Learning:** AI-generated diagrams or complex architectural visualizations often contain illegible text or "hallucinated" characters that are inaccessible to screen readers and difficult for users to interpret. Providing the documentation in a text-only format is a key accessibility win.
**Action:** Always complement visual diagrams with a structured, text-based breakdown (e.g., a "Core Components" list) to ensure accessibility, searchability, and clarity.
