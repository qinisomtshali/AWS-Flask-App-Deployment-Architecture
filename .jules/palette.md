## 2025-05-15 - Documentation Accessibility for Complex Diagrams
**Learning:** In documentation-heavy repositories, the documentation itself is the UI. Standard alt text is insufficient for complex architectural diagrams; they require structured, text-based alternatives (like 'Core Components' and 'Workflow' sections) to be fully accessible and searchable.
**Action:** When documenting architecture, always include a corresponding text-based breakdown of components and data flow to serve as a high-quality alternative to visual assets.
