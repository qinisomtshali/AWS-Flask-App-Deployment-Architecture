## 2025-05-14 - Accessible Alternatives for Complex Diagrams
**Learning:** For repositories where documentation is the primary interface, complex architectural diagrams require comprehensive text-based alternatives. Using a concise `alt` attribute that points to detailed semantic HTML/Markdown sections ensures that all users, regardless of visual ability, can access critical information.
**Action:** When adding or modifying diagrams, always provide a corresponding "Core Components" or "Workflow" section and link to it in the image's `alt` text.
