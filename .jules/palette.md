## 2024-11-21 - [Complex Diagram Accessibility]
**Learning:** Complex architecture diagrams in documentation are often invisible to screen reader users despite having brief alt text. To ensure true accessibility, documentation must provide structured text equivalents (e.g., "Core Components" lists or "Step-by-Step" workflows) that explicitly describe the visual logic.
**Action:** When documenting complex systems, always pair diagrams with a detailed text-based breakdown and update the image's `alt` attribute to guide users directly to those descriptive sections.
