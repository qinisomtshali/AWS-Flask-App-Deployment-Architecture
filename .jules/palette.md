## 2025-05-15 - Semantic Diagrams in Documentation
**Learning:** Using semantic HTML (`<figure>` and `<figcaption>`) in technical documentation improves accessibility by providing explicit context for complex diagrams that screen readers might otherwise skip or misinterpret.
**Action:** Always wrap architectural diagrams or complex technical illustrations in `<figure>` with a descriptive `<figcaption>` and performance-oriented `<img>` attributes (alt, width, height, decoding).
